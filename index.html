Frequently Asked Questions

What is a payment gateway?

A payment gateway enables businesses to accept digital payments securely, verifying customer details and facilitating fund transfers between customer accounts and merchants. It supports multiple payment modes..

What are PayU payment gateway charges?

At PayU, there are no setup or maintenance charges for the payment gateway. In case, you want to know the exact price breakup for each payment mode, kindly refer to the PayU pricing page here

Why choose PayU Payment Gateway?

PayU, a leading Indian payment gateway, supports 150+ methods, ensuring secure transactions with high success rates and PCI DSS compliance.

Does PayU payment gateway support international payments?

PayU supports global payments with Visa, MasterCard, AmEx in 150+ countries, accepting various currencies like USD, EUR, GBP, AUD, AED, SGD

In what ways can you accept digital payments through the PayU payment gateway?

PayU offers two settlement options:

  • T+2: Settlement completes two days after the transaction date.
  • Priority Settlements: Instant settlements to maintain cash flow without cash crunch worries.
